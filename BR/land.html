Pahanis / Adangal

Pahanis and Adangal records provide essential information about land ownership, cultivation, and land use. LandCare helps in obtaining, verifying, and interpreting these records to ensure accuracy and clarity.

Encumbrance Certificate (EC)

An Encumbrance Certificate confirms whether a property is free from legal or financial liabilities. We assist in obtaining and verifying ECs, helping you understand the property’s legal standing before any transaction.

Tippons

Tippons are crucial survey documents that define land boundaries and measurements. LandCare supports the retrieval and interpretation of Tippons to avoid boundary disputes and ensure proper land demarcation.

Land Maps

Land maps and survey maps help in understanding the exact location and extent of a property. We assist in obtaining village maps, survey maps, and layout plans for legal verification and planning purposes.
