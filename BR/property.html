Revenue Court Disputes

Revenue court disputes often arise due to ownership claims, land classification issues, or boundary conflicts. LandCare provides structured assistance in preparing documents, understanding revenue laws, and coordinating with concerned authorities to ensure your case is handled correctly and efficiently.

Pending Succession / Mutation

When property ownership changes due to inheritance or legal heir transfer, delays in succession or mutation can cause future disputes. We assist in completing succession formalities, updating land records, and ensuring the property is legally transferred in the rightful owner’s name.

Protected Tenancy Disputes

Protected tenancy cases involve sensitive legal rights related to long-term occupancy and tenancy laws. LandCare helps property owners and tenants understand their rights, prepare necessary documentation, and follow lawful procedures to resolve tenancy-related disputes.

Land Record Errors

Errors in land records such as incorrect names, survey numbers, or land extents can lead to serious legal issues. We support the identification and correction of these errors by coordinating with revenue offices and ensuring accurate updates in official records.

Encroachments

Encroachment of land can significantly impact property value and ownership rights. LandCare assists in identifying encroachments, gathering supporting documents, and guiding you through legal and administrative steps to resolve the issue effectively.
