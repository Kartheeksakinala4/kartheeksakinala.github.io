Who we are

The District Bar Association is the representative body of advocates practising before the courts of the district. It safeguards the professional interests of members, promotes ethical practice, and fosters cordial relations between the Bench and the Bar.

What we do

We maintain the Bar library and reading rooms, organise continuing legal education, administer a welfare fund for members and their families, and run legal aid and awareness camps across the district. Our affairs are managed by an Executive Body elected annually by the General Body.
