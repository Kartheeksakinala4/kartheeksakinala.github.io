Who we are

The District Bar Association is the representative body of advocates practising before the courts of the district. It safeguards the professional interests of its members, promotes ethical practice, and fosters cordial relations between the Bench and the Bar.

Every advocate enrolled with the State Bar Council and ordinarily practising in the district is eligible for membership.

What we do

The Association maintains the Bar library and reading rooms, organises continuing legal education, administers a welfare fund for members and their families, and coordinates legal aid and awareness camps across the district.

Its affairs are managed by an Executive Body elected each year by the General Body of members.
