Cybersecurity Services

Attackers only need one gap. We help you find every one of them first — then keep your endpoints, data, and brand continuously protected.

Vulnerability Assessment

A systematic sweep of your networks, servers, applications, and cloud assets to identify security weaknesses before they can be exploited. You get a prioritised, risk-rated report with clear remediation guidance — not a raw scanner dump.

  • Network, web, and cloud infrastructure scanning
  • Risk-rated findings mapped to business impact
  • Practical remediation roadmap and re-verification

Penetration Testing

Authorized, goal-driven simulated attacks that go beyond scanning — our testers chain weaknesses together the way a real adversary would, proving exactly what an attacker could reach and how to stop them.

  • External, internal, web app, and wireless testing
  • Manual exploitation and attack-path validation
  • Executive summary plus technical proof-of-concept detail

Endpoint Security

Laptops, desktops, and servers are where breaches begin. We design, deploy, and manage endpoint protection that detects and contains threats at the device level — before they spread across your network.

  • EDR/antivirus selection, deployment, and hardening
  • Device policy, patching, and configuration baselines
  • Threat detection, containment, and response support

Data Loss Prevention

Your most damaging breach may walk out the door in an inbox or a USB stick. We implement DLP controls that classify sensitive data and stop it from leaving your organisation — accidentally or intentionally.

  • Sensitive-data discovery and classification
  • Email, endpoint, and cloud DLP policy enforcement
  • Insider-risk monitoring and incident workflows

Brand & Dark Web Monitoring

Continuous surveillance of the open, deep, and dark web for leaked credentials, impersonation domains, counterfeit profiles, and chatter targeting your organisation — so you can act before damage is done.

  • Leaked credential and data-breach alerting
  • Phishing domain and brand-impersonation detection
  • Dark web and threat-actor forum monitoring
