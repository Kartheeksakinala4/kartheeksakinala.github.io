Digital Forensics Services

Every device tells a story. We acquire, preserve, and analyse digital evidence with strict chain-of-custody discipline — and deliver findings that stand up to scrutiny.

Computer Forensics

Forensic imaging and deep analysis of desktops, laptops, and servers — recovering deleted files, reconstructing user activity, and building defensible timelines of what happened, when, and by whom.

Learn more

Storage Media Forensics

Examination of hard drives, SSDs, USB drives, memory cards, and RAID arrays — including damaged or intentionally wiped media — to extract and validate every recoverable artifact.

Learn more

Mobile Forensics

Logical and physical extraction from Android and iOS devices — call logs, messages, chats, locations, app data, and deleted content — using industry-standard forensic tooling.

Learn more

E-mail Forensics

Header analysis, origin tracing, spoofing and phishing investigation, and mailbox reconstruction — establishing the authenticity and true source of e-mail evidence.

Learn more

Audio Forensics

Enhancement, noise reduction, and authentication of audio recordings — detecting edits, splices, and tampering, and improving intelligibility of critical conversations.

Learn more

Image Forensics

Authentication of photographs and digital images — detecting manipulation, deepfakes, and metadata inconsistencies, and recovering embedded evidence such as EXIF and geolocation data.

Learn more

Video Forensics

CCTV and video recovery, enhancement, frame-level analysis, and authentication — clarifying faces, plates, and events, and verifying whether footage has been altered.

Learn more

OSINT

Open-source intelligence investigations across social media, public records, and online platforms — profiling threats, verifying identities, and gathering verifiable intelligence ethically and legally.

Learn more

Malware Forensics

Static and dynamic analysis of malicious code — identifying what the malware did, what data it touched, how it got in, and the indicators you need to eradicate it and prevent reinfection.

Learn more

Network Forensics

Capture and analysis of network traffic, logs, and flow data to reconstruct intrusions, trace exfiltration, and attribute attacks — turning packets into a clear narrative of the incident.

Learn more
