Endpoint Security

Detect and contain threats on laptops, desktops, and servers — before they spread.

Cybersecurity

Overview

Most breaches begin on an endpoint: a phishing click, a malicious download, an unpatched laptop. Endpoint security puts detection and response capability directly on every device, so threats are caught and contained at the point of entry.

We help you choose the right EDR/antivirus platform for your budget, deploy and harden it correctly, and set up the policies and response workflows that make it actually effective.

Essential for any organisation with remote or hybrid workers, and the single highest-impact control for small and mid-sized businesses.

What you get

  • EDR / antivirus selection, deployment, and tuning
  • Device hardening and configuration baselines
  • Patch management strategy and enforcement
  • USB and removable-media control policies
  • Threat detection, containment, and response support
  • Ongoing managed monitoring options
