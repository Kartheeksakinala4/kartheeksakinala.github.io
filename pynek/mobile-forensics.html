Mobile Forensics

Logical and physical extraction from Android and iOS devices.

Digital Forensics

Overview

Phones are the richest evidence source in most modern investigations: messages, chats, calls, photos, locations, app data, and deleted content. Mobile forensics extracts and decodes this data using industry-standard tooling.

We support Android and iOS with logical and physical extraction, examining everything from WhatsApp and Telegram artifacts to GPS history and cloud backups — always with documented chain of custody.

Central to matrimonial disputes, employee investigations, fraud cases, and law-enforcement support.

What you get

  • Logical and physical extraction (Android & iOS)
  • Deleted message and media recovery
  • Chat application artifact analysis (WhatsApp, Telegram, Signal and more)
  • Location history and movement mapping
  • Cloud backup acquisition where lawful
  • Detailed extraction reports
