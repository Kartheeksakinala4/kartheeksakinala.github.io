Video Forensics

CCTV recovery, enhancement, and authentication — frame by frame.

Digital Forensics

Overview

Video forensics turns poor-quality footage into usable evidence: recovering deleted CCTV, clarifying faces and number plates, stabilising shaky footage, and verifying that video has not been altered.

We work with DVR/NVR systems, proprietary CCTV formats, body cameras, and phone video, preserving original evidence integrity throughout.

Vital in accident reconstruction, theft and assault cases, and workplace incident investigations.

What you get

  • Deleted / overwritten CCTV recovery from DVRs & NVRs
  • Face, object, and number-plate clarification
  • Frame-by-frame event analysis
  • Tampering and edit detection
  • Format conversion from proprietary CCTV systems
  • Detailed exhibits and reports
