Digital Forensics & Internal Investigations

Digital forensics is not always about court cases or major incidents. Many businesses need quiet clarity — to understand what happened and decide next steps without escalation.

Employee Misuse & Internal Investigation Support

Designed for HR teams, administrators, and business owners dealing with sensitive internal issues such as data misuse, policy violations, or suspected insider activity.

  • Email misuse and access review
  • USB and file transfer activity checks
  • System access timeline reconstruction

Focus on facts and clarity — not accusations or legal escalation.

Pre-Litigation Digital Evidence Preservation

Before any legal decision is made, digital evidence must be preserved correctly. This service ensures data is protected, verified, and usable if needed later.

  • Laptop and desktop forensic imaging
  • Email and cloud data preservation
  • Hash-verified documentation

Ideal when you want to preserve first and decide later.

A preventive audit — not recovery after failure.

Online Exposure & Executive Risk Monitoring

Cybersecurity Services (SME-Focused)

Cybersecurity should not begin with expensive tools. It should begin with understanding and prioritisation. I help you improve what you already have before recommending anything new.

Cybersecurity Readiness Assessment

A short, practical engagement designed to give you a clear view of your current security posture and highest-priority actions.

  • Network exposure review
  • Firewall configuration sanity check
  • Backup and endpoint hygiene review
  • Policy and process gap identification

Deliverable: clear risk summary with priority actions — not a lengthy technical report.

Firewall Rule Review & Cleanup

Many SMEs already have firewalls that were installed years ago and never reviewed. This service focuses on improving what you already have.
